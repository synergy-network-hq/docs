**Effective Date:** September 23, 2025

- Users are responsible for transaction fees and ensuring they follow the correct payment process.

## 3. Refund Exceptions

Refunds may be considered only in cases of:

- Technical error on the platform preventing the successful transfer of assets.

All refund requests must be submitted with evidence of the issue and will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. LightMeme AI reserves the sole right to approve or deny refunds.

## 4. Delivery of Digital Assets

- Digital assets purchased are delivered via blockchain transfer to the address provided by the user at the time of purchase.
- Users are solely responsible for providing correct wallet addresses. LightMeme AI is not liable for assets sent to an incorrect address.

## 5. Risk Acknowledgment

- Users acknowledge that the value of tokens and NFTs may fluctuate.

# LightMeme AI - Security and Data Breach Policy

**Effective Date:** September 23, 2025  
**Last Updated:** September 23, 2025

LightMeme AI, LLC ("LightMeme AI") is committed to protecting user data and maintaining platform security. This policy outlines how we safeguard information and respond to potential breaches.

## 1. Security Measures

- Encryption of sensitive data, including wallet addresses and transaction history.
- Multi-layer access controls for internal systems.
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
- Secure backups and disaster recovery protocols.

## 2. User Responsibilities

Users must:

- Protect their account credentials and private keys.
- Avoid sharing sensitive information publicly.
- Report suspicious activity immediately to LightMeme AI support.

## 3. Data Breach Response

- In the event of a data breach, LightMeme AI will investigate promptly and contain the incident.
- Affected users will be notified in accordance with applicable laws.
- Remediation steps, including password resets, enhanced monitoring, or security updates, will be implemented as needed.

## 4. Limitation

- LightMeme AI is not responsible for breaches caused by user negligence, third-party services, or force majeure events.

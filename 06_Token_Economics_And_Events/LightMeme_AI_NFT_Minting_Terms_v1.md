# NFT Minting Terms
## Synergy Network

**Effective Date:** September 26, 2025 | **Last Updated:** September 26, 2025

These terms govern the minting of Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) on the Synergy Network platform. By minting an NFT, you agree to these terms.

---

## 1. Minting Process

- **How to Mint:** Users can mint NFTs from available collections by connecting their wallet and paying the mint price in supported cryptocurrencies (e.g., ETH).
- **Supply:** Each collection has a limited supply; minting stops when sold out.
- **Smart Contracts:** Minting is executed via blockchain smart contracts. You are responsible for gas fees.
- **Confirmation:** Successful mints are recorded on the blockchain; check your wallet for the NFT.

---

## 2. Ownership and Rights

- **Ownership:** Upon successful minting, you own the NFT as a unique digital asset and can transfer, sell, or display it.
- **Intellectual Property:** The underlying artwork or media may be subject to creator rights. Synergy Network does not claim ownership of minted NFTs but retains rights to platform tools and features.
- **Commercial Use:** You may use your NFT for personal or commercial purposes, subject to any collection-specific licenses.

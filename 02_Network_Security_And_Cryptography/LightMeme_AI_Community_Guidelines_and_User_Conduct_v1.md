# Community Guidelines and User Conduct

**Effective Date:** September 23, 2025 | **Last Updated:** September 23, 2025

Synergy Network ("Synergy Network") strives to maintain a safe, inclusive, and respectful community. All users ("you", "your") must adhere to these guidelines when interacting on the platform, its forums, or affiliated channels.

## 1. Prohibited Conduct

You agree not to:

- Engage in harassment, hate speech, threats, or abusive behavior.
- Post or distribute illegal, fraudulent, or harmful content.
- Attempt to manipulate, exploit, or scam other users.
- Share private information of other users without consent.
- Circumvent platform security, moderation, or enforcement mechanisms.

---

## 3. Enforcement

- Synergy Network may remove content, suspend, or terminate accounts at its sole discretion for violations.
- Repeated violations may result in permanent bans or legal action.

---

## 4. Reporting

- Users are encouraged to report violations via the official reporting mechanisms.
- Synergy Network will review reports and take appropriate action.

---

## 5. Acknowledgment

By using Synergy Network services, you agree to abide by these Community Guidelines and understand that violation may result in restricted access or termination of your account.

# LightMeme AI - NFT Minting Terms

**Effective Date:** September 26, 2025  

**Last Updated:** September 26, 2025

These terms govern the minting of Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) on the LightMeme AI platform. By minting an NFT, you agree to these terms.

## 1. Minting Process

- **How to Mint:** Users can mint NFTs from available collections by connecting their wallet and paying the mint price in supported cryptocurrencies (e.g., ETH).
- **Supply:** Each collection has a limited supply; minting stops when sold out.
- **Smart Contracts:** Minting is executed via blockchain smart contracts. You are responsible for gas fees.
- **Confirmation:** Successful mints are recorded on the blockchain; check your wallet for the NFT.

## 2. Ownership and Rights

- **Ownership:** Upon successful minting, you own the NFT as a unique digital asset and can transfer, sell, or display it.
- **Intellectual Property:** The underlying artwork or media may be subject to creator rights. LightMeme AI does not claim ownership of minted NFTs but retains rights to platform tools and features.
- **Commercial Use:** You may use your NFT for personal or commercial purposes, subject to any collection-specific licenses.

## 3. Royalties and Fees

- **Minting Fee:** A fee is charged for minting, part of which may go to the creator and platform.
- **Secondary Sales:** Royalties (e.g., 5-10%) are automatically collected on secondary market sales via smart contracts.
- **Platform Fees:** Additional fees may apply for using marketplace features.

## 4. Prohibitions

- Minting for fraudulent, illegal, or infringing purposes is prohibited.
- You must comply with all applicable laws, including AML/KYC requirements.
- No spamming or abusive minting behavior.

## 5. Risks

- **Blockchain Risks:** Transactions are irreversible; lost NFTs cannot be recovered.
- **Market Risks:** NFT value can fluctuate; no guarantees.
- **Technical Risks:** Smart contract bugs or network issues may affect minting.

## 6. Dispute Resolution

Disputes related to minting will be resolved under the platform's general terms.

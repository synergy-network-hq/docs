## 3. Locked Token Sale Architecture

All token sale participants receive a locked SNRG entitlement token issued on the Base network. These locked tokens:

- Are non-transferable
- Cannot be traded on secondary markets
- Do not contribute to circulating supply
- Represent a vesting-controlled claim on future native SNRG

## 5. Investor Vesting & Unlock Schedules

### 5.1 Early Supporters ($0.0026)
- **Instrument:** Locked Base token
- **TGE Unlock:** 5%
- **Cliff:** 6 months
- **Remaining:** Linear vesting over 24 months
- **Redemption:** Gradual migration to native SNRG

### 5.2 Early Growth Round ($0.0038)
- **Instrument:** Locked Base token
- **TGE Unlock:** 7.5%
- **Cliff:** 3 months
- **Remaining:** Linear vesting over 18 months

### 5.3 Strategic / Private Investors
- **Instrument:** Locked Base token
- **TGE Unlock:** 10%
- **Cliff:** 3 months
- **Remaining:** Linear vesting over 12–18 months (round-specific)

### 5.4 Public Sale Participants
- **Instrument:** Locked Base token
- **TGE Unlock:** 15–20%
- **Remaining:** Linear vesting over 6 months

All sale participants are subject to lockups. There are no unrestricted early allocations.

## 6. Team & Support Vesting

## 9. Ecosystem, DAO, and Treasury Controls

**Ecosystem / Developer Incentives:** Grants, tooling, and infrastructure support; released based on measurable contribution milestones.

**DAO / Governance Reserve:** Protocol upgrades, emergency actions, and long-term governance initiatives.

**Foundation / Treasury:** Operational runway, legal/compliance/research expenses, infrastructure and security investment.

All are subject to governance oversight and multi-sig controls.

---

## 10. Circulating Supply Discipline

At network launch and public listing:

- Majority of total supply remains non-circulating
- Early sale tokens are locked
- Team allocation is fully cliffed
- Validator emissions are not yet active

This structure ensures controlled price discovery, reduced volatility, and predictable supply introduction.

---

## 11. Summary

Synergy Network's tokenomics prioritize long-term network health over short-term liquidity. All participants — team, investors, and ecosystem contributors — are aligned through enforced vesting, transparent unlock schedules, and governance-controlled supply mechanisms. SNRG is engineered to support a durable, decentralized network economy rather than speculative churn.

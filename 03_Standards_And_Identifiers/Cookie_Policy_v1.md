# Cookie Policy
## Synergy Network

**Effective Date:** September 23, 2025

Synergy Network ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website to provide an optimized browsing experience, analyze website performance, deliver personalized content, and comply with regulatory obligations.

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, analyze usage, and provide a more personalized user experience.

---

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### 2.1 Necessary Cookies

Essential for the website to function — enabling account login, session management, privacy settings, and security compliance. Cannot be disabled.

### 2.2 Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our site. Collect anonymized information about page visits, user behavior, and performance. Examples: Plausible, Hotjar.

### 2.3 Preferences Cookies

Store your choices such as language and display preferences, allowing the website to remember your settings.

### 2.4 Marketing Cookies

Track browsing habits to deliver targeted content and advertisements. Examples: AdRoll and other third-party ad services.

---

## 3. How We Use Cookies

| Purpose | Description |
|---|---|
| **Service Delivery** | Enable core website functionality |
| **Performance and Analytics** | Monitor usage patterns and optimize performance |
| **Marketing and Personalization** | Provide targeted content, offers, and promotions |
| **Compliance** | Ensure compliance with legal requirements and security standards |

---

## 4. Your Choices

You may manage your cookie preferences at any time through our cookie banner or settings modal:

- Accepting all cookies
- Rejecting all non-essential cookies
- Selecting specific cookie categories to allow or disallow

Disabling cookies may affect certain features or the website's functionality.

---

## 5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share anonymized analytics and marketing data with third-party providers such as Plausible, Hotjar, and AdRoll. These providers adhere to strict privacy standards and cannot use data beyond the specified purpose.

---

## 6. Storage and Retention

Cookies are stored on your device and remain until they expire or are deleted. Cookie consent preferences are stored locally for record-keeping and compliance purposes.

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated through our website. Continued use of our platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
